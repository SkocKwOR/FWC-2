\item Number of heads in two tosses of a coin.\\
 By using binomial distribution
 \begin{align}
X_1 &= k\\ 
n &=2\\ 
  P_{X_1}(k) &=
    \begin{cases}
      \comb{n}{k}p^{k}q^{n-k}, & \text{ 0$\leq$k$\leq$ 2}\\
    \end{cases}             
\end{align}
